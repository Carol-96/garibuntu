About Us

Garibuntu is a platform dedicated to uniting car enthusiasts, providing a space for sharing knowledge, organizing events, and supporting communities. Whether you're a member of a car club or a sponsor looking to support a worthy cause, Garibuntu brings everyone together under one hub.

We also bring you closer to trusted garages, spareparts traders, car importers, car reviewers, and mechanics for your specific type of car. Indeed, we are a hub that makes your build journey seamless and keeps you closer to a community with people you share the same passion.
